Alferdo Pasta

Alfredo Pasta is a classic Italian dish that consists of perfectly cooked pasta coated in a creamy and indulgent Alfredo sauce. The sauce is made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese, resulting in a rich and velvety texture. This decadent pasta dish is often garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs, adding a burst of flavor to each bite.
