BBQ Club Sandwich

The BBQ Club Sandwich is a mouthwatering creation that combines layers of grilled or roasted chicken, crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and creamy mayonnaise, all stacked between toasted bread slices. What sets it apart is the addition of tangy and smoky barbecue sauce, which adds a delicious flavor kick to this hearty and satisfying sandwich option.
