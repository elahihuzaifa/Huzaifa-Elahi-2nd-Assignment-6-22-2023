Chatkhara Tikka

Chatkhara Tikka is a flavorful variation of Chicken Tikka that incorporates tangy and spicy flavors. The chicken pieces are marinated with a blend of aromatic spices, including cumin, coriander, chili powder, and chaat masala, which gives it a tangy and chatpata (savory and tangy) taste. The marinated chicken is then grilled or roasted to perfection, resulting in a tantalizing dish with a delightful combination of spices and flavors.
