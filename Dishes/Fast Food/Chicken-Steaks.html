Chicken Steaks

Chicken Steaks are a popular dish consisting of boneless chicken breasts that are marinated, seasoned, and cooked to perfection. The chicken is typically tenderized, seasoned with herbs and spices, and then grilled, pan-seared, or baked until juicy and flavorful. Chicken steaks are often served with a variety of side dishes such as mashed potatoes, grilled vegetables, or a fresh salad, making it a versatile and satisfying meal option for chicken lovers.
