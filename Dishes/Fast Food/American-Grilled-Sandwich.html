American Grilled Sandwich

The American Grilled Sandwich is a classic comfort food that features a filling of ingredients such as sliced ham, turkey, cheese, and sometimes bacon, between two slices of bread. It is then toasted or grilled until the cheese melts and the bread turns golden and crispy, resulting in a warm and satisfying handheld meal option.
