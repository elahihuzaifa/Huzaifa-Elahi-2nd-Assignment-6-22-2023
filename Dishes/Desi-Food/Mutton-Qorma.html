Mutton Qorma

Mutton Qorma is a flavorful and rich meat dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It is traditionally prepared by slow-cooking tender pieces of mutton (goat or lamb) in a spiced yogurt-based gravy. The word "Qorma" refers to the cooking technique of braising or simmering meat in a liquid until it becomes tender and infused with the flavors of the spices.
