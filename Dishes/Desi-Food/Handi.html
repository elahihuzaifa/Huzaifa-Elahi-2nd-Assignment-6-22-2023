Paneer Makhni Hadni

Paneer Makhani, also known as Paneer Butter Masala, is a popular vegetarian dish originating from North Indian cuisine. It consists of soft and creamy cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a rich and flavorful tomato-based gravy.

 Chicken Paneer Reshmi Handi

Chicken Paneer Reshmi is a delectable fusion dish that combines tender chicken and creamy paneer in a rich and flavorful marinade. It offers a delightful blend of textures and flavors, making it a popular choice among meat and cheese enthusiasts.
