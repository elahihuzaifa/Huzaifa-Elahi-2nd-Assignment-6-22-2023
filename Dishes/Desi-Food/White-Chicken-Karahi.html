Chicken Karahi

White Chicken Karahi

White Chicken Karahi, also known as Chicken Karahi (without tomatoes), is a popular Pakistani and North Indian dish characterized by its creamy white gravy and flavorful spices. It is a variation of the traditional Chicken Karahi, where the tomatoes are omitted, resulting in a lighter and milder version of the dish.
