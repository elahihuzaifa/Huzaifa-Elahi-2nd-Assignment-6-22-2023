Mutton Karahi

Mutton Karahi is a popular Pakistani and North Indian dish featuring tender pieces of mutton (goat or lamb) cooked in a flavorful and aromatic gravy. The meat is marinated in a blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, and garam masala. It is then cooked in a karahi or a deep pan with caramelized onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and fresh ginger. The result is a succulent mutton dish with a thick and rich gravy that is typically served with naan bread or rice.

 Brain Tawa Masala with butter Karahi

Brain Tawa Masala with Butter Karahi is a delectable and indulgent dish that features brain (typically goat or lamb brain) cooked in a flavorful and buttery masala gravy. It is a popular delicacy in Pakistani and Indian cuisine.
